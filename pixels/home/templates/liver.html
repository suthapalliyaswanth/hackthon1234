Liver Function

The liver detoxifies the body by removing toxins, metabolizes nutrients from food, and produces bile to aid in digestion.

Liver Toxic

Liver toxicity occurs when harmful substances overload the liver's capacity to detoxify, leading to potential damage and impaired function, compromising overall health.

Damge of Alcohol

Alcohol can cause inflammation, fatty liver disease, hepatitis, and cirrhosis, progressively damaging liver cells and impairing liver function over time
