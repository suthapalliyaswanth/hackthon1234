HEART INFORMATION

Functonal Of Heart

The heart functions as the body's central pump, propelling blood throughout the circulatory system to deliver vital oxygen and nutrients to tissues while removing waste products.

Normal Vs Problem

A normal heart pumps blood smoothly, keeping your body healthy. But if there's a problem with the heart, it might not pump well, which can make you feel sick.

Heart Stock

A heart attack happens when something blocks the flow of blood to the heart, causing damage to the heart muscle.
