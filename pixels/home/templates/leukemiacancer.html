Leukemia Cancer Treament

Treatment for leukemia cancer depends on the type of leukemia, its subtype, the patient's age and overall health, and other factors. Common treatments may include chemotherapy, targeted therapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy, stem cell transplantation, or a combination of these approaches. The goal of treatment is to destroy leukemia cells, induce remission, and prevent recurrence. Treatment plans are personalized and may involve different phases such as induction, consolidation, and maintenance therapy. Close monitoring and follow-up care are important for managing leukemia and optimizing outcomes.
