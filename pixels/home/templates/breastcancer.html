Breastcancer Symptoms

Breast cancer symptoms can include a lump or thickening in the breast or underarm, changes in breast size or shape, nipple discharge or inversion, skin dimpling or redness, and persistent breast pain. Early detection through self-examinations, mammograms, and regular screenings improves treatment success rates and prognosis.

Breast Cancer Treatment

Breast cancer treatment typically involves a combination of surgery, chemotherapy, radiation therapy, hormone therapy, and targeted therapy, tailored to the individual's specific cancer stage, type, and overall health, with the goal of removing or destroying cancer cells and preventing recurrence. Early detection and multidisciplinary approaches enhance treatment effectiveness and survival rates.
