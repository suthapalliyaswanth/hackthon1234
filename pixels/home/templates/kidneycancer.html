Kidney Cancer Symptoms

Symptoms of kidney cancer may include blood in the urine, persistent pain in the side or back, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, and intermittent fevers, often manifesting in advanced stages when the cancer has spread. Early detection through routine check-ups is crucial for better prognosis and treatment outcomes.

Kidney Cancer Treatment

Treatment for kidney cancer often involves a combination of surgery, targeted therapy, immunotherapy, or radiation therapy, tailored to the stage and type of cancer, with regular monitoring to manage and prevent recurrence. Beyond surgery, treatments like radiation therapy and chemotherapy may be employed to target any remaining cancer cells, while newer approaches like immunotherapy harness the body's immune system to fight the cancer.
