Lymphoma cancer

Symptoms of lymphoma cancer can vary depending on the type and location of the cancer, but they may include swollen lymph nodes, fever, night sweats, unexplained weight loss, fatigue, itching, and enlarged spleen or liver. Other symptoms can include chest pain, difficulty breathing, abdominal pain or swelling, and recurrent infections. Early diagnosis through medical evaluation and diagnostic tests such as imaging studies and biopsies is essential for timely treatment and better outcomes.

Lympho Cancer Treament

Treatment for lymphoma cancer depends on various factors including the type, stage, and individual patient factors. Common treatment options may include chemotherapy, radiation therapy, immunotherapy, targeted therapy, stem cell transplantation.
